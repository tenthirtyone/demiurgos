True Ownership

Character and rare item data are stored on the Ethereum blockchain as ERC-721 NFT tokens. Players can freely trade their characters and items they find in the game at the player marketplace.

Anti-Cheating

In-game trade is protected by the same technology that secures billions of dollars of cryptocurrency. When buying or selling, players can verify the authenticity of every game item. Transactions cannot be reversed. Once you purchase an item or character the previous owner cannot steal it back.

Cross-Game Interoperability

Character data will carry over to every game in the Demiurgos series. Late-game quests will require players to move their characters between games to find the rarest items.

Community Focused

We want your helping building the Demiurgos Universe. That's why we chose to make character and item data public so that players and other developers could build for the community without our permission.

No Cryptocurrency Required

No Rube-Goldberg coin economics that force players purchase or use a custom cryptocurrency, protocol, ecosystem or platform. Just play the game. We'll pay the fees and manage the blockchain and you own your keys.

Play To Earn

The current status quo of Freemium gaming is a pay-to-win scheme that favors bank balances over player skill and dedication. Demiurgos charges a single fee to create a character on the Ethereum blockchain and will not sell in-game items that give one player advantage over others.

Demiurgos is a text-based roleplaying game similar to Legend of the Red Dragon (LORD) launching in 2020. In Demiurgos, players chose one of four demigods to fight, conquer, and quest to control the game universe. Demiurgos is not play-to-win, all characters and items are created by players during the course of the game. Character data are stored on the Ethereum blockchain and owned entirely by players for a truly player-driven economy.

The Demiurgos series is designed to span multiple, co-existing, games and genres to be the first game of its kind that's only possible with blockchain. The first player to reach level 100 in Demiurgos will determine the starting conditions and game-wide effects for the next game in the Demiurgos Universe.
